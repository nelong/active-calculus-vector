Activity 9.4.3.

  1. Find the area of the parallelogram formed by the vectors \(\vu = \langle 1,3, -2\rangle\) and \(\vv=\langle 3,0,1\rangle\text{.}\)
  2. Find the area of the parallelogram in \(\R^3\) whose vertices are \((1,0,1)\text{,}\) \((0,0,1)\text{,}\) \((2,1,0)\text{,}\) and \((1,1,0)\text{.}\) (Hint: It might be helpful to draw a picture to see how the vertices are arranged so you can determine which vectors you might use.)
