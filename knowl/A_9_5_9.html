Activity 9.5.4.

  1. Write a scalar equation of the plane \(p_1\) passing through the point \((0, 2, 4)\) and perpendicular to the vector \(\vn=\langle 2, -1, 1\rangle\text{.}\)
  2. Is the point \((2, 0, 2)\) on the plane \(p_1\text{?}\)
  3. Write a scalar equation of the plane \(p_2\) that is parallel to \(p_1\) and passing through the point \((3, 0, 4)\text{.}\) (Hint: Compare normal vectors of the planes.)
  4. Write a parametric description of the line \(l\) passing through the point \((2,0,2)\) and perpendicular to the plane \(p_3\) described by the equation \(x+2y-2z = 7\text{.}\)
  5. Find the point at which \(l\) intersects the plane \(p_3\text{.}\)
