Exercise 13.

Let \(\vv = \langle 1, -2 \rangle\text{,}\) \(\vu = \langle 0, 4 \rangle\text{,}\) and \(\vw = \langle -5, 7 \rangle\text{.}\)

  1. Determine the components of the vector \(\vu - \vv\text{.}\)

  2. Determine the components of the vector \(2\vv - 3\vu\text{.}\)

  3. Determine the components of the vector \(\vv + 2\vu - 7 \vw\text{.}\)

  4. Determine scalars \(a\) and \(b\) such that \(a \vv + b\vu = \vw\text{.}\) Show all of your work in finding \(a\) and \(b\text{.}\)
