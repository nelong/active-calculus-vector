Exercise 15.

For each of the following, describe the effect of the parameter \(s\) on the parametric curve for \(t\) in the interval \([0,2 \pi]\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle \vr(t) = \langle \cos(t), \sin(t) + s \rangle\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \vr(t) = \langle \cos(t)-s, \sin(t) \rangle\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \vr(t) = \langle s\cos(t), \sin(t) \rangle\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \vr(t) = \langle s\cos(t), s\sin(t) \rangle\)

  5. \(\displaystyle \vr(t) = \langle \cos(st), \sin(st) \rangle\)
