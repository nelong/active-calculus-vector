Exercise 10.

Are the following statements true or false?
  1. \((\vec{i} \times \vec{j}) \cdot \vec{k} = \vec{i} \cdot (\vec{j} \times \vec{k})\text{.}\)
  2. If \(\vec{v}\) and \(\vec{w}\) are any two vectors, then \(||\vec{v} + \vec{w}|| = ||\vec{v}|| + ||\vec{w}||\text{.}\)
  3. For any scalar \(c\) and any vector \(\vec{v}\text{,}\) we have \(||c\vec{v}|| = c ||\vec{v}||\text{.}\)
  4. The value of \(\vec{v} \cdot (\vec{v} \times \vec{w})\) is always zero.
