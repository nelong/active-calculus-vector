Answer 12.1.6.2.1.

The answer. Keep this simple as will be made public “back of the book”-style. Do not put anything with a number/or @xml:id on it in an <answer> or <solution>, because this will mess up the numbering of subsequent things in the book. Technically @xml:id is fine, but note that the reference might not be useful in the end. Definitely don't use <figure> or <table> in one of these things; wrap <tabular> and <image> in <sidebyside>.
