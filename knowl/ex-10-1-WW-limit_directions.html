\(\lim\limits_{{}^{(x,y)\to(0,0)}_{y=x}} f(x,y) =\)

(b) Find the limit of \(f\) as \((x,y)\to(0,0)\) along the curve \(y = x^4\text{.}\)

\(\lim\limits_{{}^{(x,y)\to(0,0)}_{y=x^4}} f(x,y) =\)
