Exercise 8.

Are the following statements true or false?

  1. A parametrization of the graph of \(y = \ln(x)\) for \(x > 0\) is given by \(x = e^t, y = t\) for \(-\infty \lt t \lt \infty\text{.}\)

  2. The parametric curve \(x = (3t+4)^2, y = 5(3t+4)^2-9\text{,}\) for \(0 \leq t \leq 3\) is a line segment.

  3. The line parametrized by \(x = 7, y = 5t, z = 6 + t\) is parallel to the x-axis.
