Exercise 11.

For each of the following iterated integrals,

  • sketch the region of integration,

  • write an equivalent iterated integral expression in the opposite order of integration,

  • choose one of the two orders and evaluate the integral.

  1. \(\displaystyle \int_{x=0}^{x=1} \int_{y=x^2}^{y=x} xy \, dy \, dx\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \int_{y=0}^{y=2} \int_{x=-\sqrt{4-y^2}}^{x=0} xy \, dx \, dy\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \int_{x=0}^{x=1} \int_{y=x^4}^{y=x^{1/4}} x+y \, dy \, dx\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \int_{y=0}^{y=2} \int_{x=y/2}^{x=2y} x+y \, dx \, dy\)
