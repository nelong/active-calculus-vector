We say that a vector field \(\vF\) defined on a region \(D\) is path-independent if \(\int_{C_1}\vF\cdot d\vr = \int_{C_2}\vF\cdot d\vr\) whenever \(C_1\) and \(C_2\) are oriented paths in \(D\) such that both curves start at point \(P\) and end at point \(Q\text{.}\)
