Item b.iii.
Finally, now that we have thought about slicing up the two-dimensional domain that is the projection of the cone, what are the limits on \(z\) in the innermost integral? Note that over any point \((x,y)\) in the plane, a vertical slice in the \(z\) direction will involve a range of values from the cone itself to its flat top. In particular, observe that at least one of these limits is not constant but depends on \(x\) and \(y\text{.}\)
