Item 12.8.5.7.b.
If we let \(C = C_1 + C_2\text{,}\) then the orientation of \(C\) is as required by Green's Theorem—when walking along the pieces of \(C\text{,}\) the region \(R\) is always on the left-hand side. Assume that \(\vF\) is a vector field that is smooth on \(R\) and \(C\text{.}\) Using Figure 12.8.8 as a guide, write \(\oint_C \vF\cdot d\vr\) as a sum of double integrals, one over \(R_1\) and the other over \(R_2\text{.}\)
