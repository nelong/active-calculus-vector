Item b.

We know one point on the tangent plane; namely, the \(z\)-value of the tangent plane agrees with the \(z\)-value on the graph of \(f(x,y) = 6 - \frac{x^2}2 - y^2\) at the point \((x_0, y_0)\text{.}\) In other words, both the tangent plane and the graph of the function \(f\) contain the point \((x_0, y_0, z_0)\text{.}\) Use this observation to determine \(z_0\) in the expression \(z = z_0 + a(x-x_0) + b(y-y_0)\text{.}\)
