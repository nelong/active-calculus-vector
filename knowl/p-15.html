Recognizing that not all institutions will cover all the material in this chapter, we have intended that the overview of vector fields, line integrals of vector fields, and Fundamental Theorem of Calculus for Line Integrals (Section 12.1–12.4) can be combined with Section 12.8 on Green’s Theorem. We would encourage you to explore the section on curl before proceeding to Green’s Theorem, but only the first portion on circulation density would be terribly beneficial. Section 12.7 does make some passing backward references to the development of divergence in Section 12.6, but it is not necessary to cover divergence in order to do the basics of circulation density and then Green’s Theorem.
