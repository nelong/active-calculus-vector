Consider the function \(f\) defined by \(f(x,y) = \frac{xy}{x^2 + y^2 + 1}.\)
  1. What is the domain of \(f\text{?}\)
  2. Evaluate limit of \(f\) at \((0,0)\) along the following paths: \(x = 0\text{,}\) \(y = 0\text{,}\) \(y = x\text{,}\) and \(y = x^2\text{.}\)
  3. What do you conjecture is the value of \(\lim_{(x,y) \to (0,0)} f(x,y)\text{?}\)
  4. Is \(f\) continuous at \((0,0)\text{?}\) Why or why not?
  5. Use appropriate technology to sketch both surface and contour plots of \(f\) near \((0,0)\text{.}\) Write several sentences to say how your plots affirm your findings in (a) - (d).
