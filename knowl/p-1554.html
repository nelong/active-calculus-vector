Consider the function \(g\) defined by \(g(x,y) = \frac{xy}{x^2 + y^2}.\)

  1. What is the domain of \(g\text{?}\)

  2. Evaluate limit of \(g\) at \((0,0)\) along the following paths: \(x = 0\text{,}\) \(y = x\text{,}\) and \(y = 2x\text{.}\)

  3. What can you now say about the value of \(\lim_{(x,y) \to (0,0)} g(x,y)\text{?}\)

  4. Is \(g\) continuous at \((0,0)\text{?}\) Why or why not?

  5. Use appropriate technology to sketch both surface and contour plots of \(g\) near \((0,0)\text{.}\) Write several sentences to say how your plots affirm your findings in (a) - (d).
