Suppose we are at the point \((x_0,y_0)\text{,}\) and we know the value \(f(x_0,y_0)\) of \(f\) at \((x_0,y_0)\text{.}\) If we consider the displacement \(\langle \Delta x, \Delta y\rangle\) to a new point \((x,y) = (x_0+\Delta x, y_0 + \Delta y)\text{,}\) we would like to know how much the function has changed. We denote this change by \(\Delta f\text{,}\) where
\begin{equation*} \Delta f = f(x,y) - f(x_0, y_0). \end{equation*}
