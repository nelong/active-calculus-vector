Let \(f(x,y) = 3xy-x^2y^3\text{.}\)
  1. Determine \(f_x(x,y)\) and \(f_y(x,y)\text{.}\)
  2. Use Equation (10.6.3) to determine \(D_{\vi} f(x,y)\) and \(D_{\vj} f(x,y)\text{.}\) What familiar function is \(D_{\vi} f\text{?}\) What familiar function is \(D_{\vj} f\text{?}\) (Recall that \(\vi\) is the unit vector in the positive \(x\)-direction and \(\vj\) is the unit vector in the positive \(y\)-direction.)
  3. Use Equation (10.6.3) to find the derivative of \(f\) in the direction of the vector \(\vv = \langle 2, 3 \rangle\) at the point \((1,-1)\text{.}\) Remember that a unit direction vector is needed.
