Evaluate each of the following double or iterated integrals exactly.

  1. \(\displaystyle \int_1^3 \left( \int_2^5 xy \, dy \right) \, dx\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \int_0^{\pi/4} \left( \int_0^{\pi/3} \sin(x) \cos(y) \, dx \right) \, dy\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \int_0^1 \left( \int_0^1 e^{-2x - 3y} \, dy \right) \, dx\)

  4. \(\iint_R \sqrt{2x + 5y} \, dA\text{,}\) where \(R = [0,2]\times[0,3]\text{.}\)
