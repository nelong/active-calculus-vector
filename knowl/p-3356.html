The center of mass, \((\overline{x},\overline{y})\text{,}\) of a continuous lamina with a variable density \(\delta(x,y)\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \overline{x} = \frac{\iint_D x\delta(x,y) \, dA}{\iint_D \delta(x,y) \, dA} \ \ \ \ \ \text{ and } \ \ \ \ \ \overline{y} = \frac{\iint_D y\delta(x,y) \, dA}{\iint_D \delta(x,y) \, dA}. \end{equation*}
