By properties of line integrals, we know that \(\int_C \vF\cdot d\vr = -\int_{-C}\vF\cdot d\vr\text{,}\) and we will use this property since \(-C\) is the usual clockwise orientation of a circle, meaning we can parametrize \(-C\) by \(\vr(t) = \langle 3\cos(t),3\sin(t)\rangle\) for \(0\leq t\leq \pi/2\text{.}\)
