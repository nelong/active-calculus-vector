The typical parametrization of the line segment from \((0,1)\) to \((3,3)\) (the oriented curve \(C_3\) in Example 12.3.8) is \(\vr(t) = \langle 3t,1+2t\rangle\) where \(0 \leq t\leq 1\text{.}\) Use this parametrization to calculate \(\int_{C_3}\vF\cdot d\vr\) for the vector field \(\vF = x\vi\) and compare your answer to the result of Example 12.3.8.
