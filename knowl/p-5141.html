By considering the circulation density of a three-dimensional vector field \(\vF = \langle F_1,F_2,F_2\rangle\) in planes parallel to the coordinate planes, we can calculate the curl of \(\vF\) as
\begin{equation*} \curl(\vF)= \left(\frac{\partial F_3}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial F_2}{\partial z}\right)\vi- \left(\frac{\partial F_3}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial F_1}{\partial z}\right)\vj + \left(\frac{\partial F_2}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial F_1}{\partial y}\right)\vk\text{.} \end{equation*}
