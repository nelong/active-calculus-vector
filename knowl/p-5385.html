Using the vector fields from above, note that the line integrals along the legs of the triangle are \(0\) for both \(\vF\) and \(\vG\text{.}\) Thus, we parametrize the hypotenuse as \(\vr(t) = \langle a(1-t),bt\rangle\) with \(0\leq t\leq 1\text{.}\) The area of the triangle is \(ab/2\text{,}\) so we find
\begin{gather*} \overline{x} = \frac{1}{ab}\int_0^1 a^2b(1-t)^2\, dt = \frac{a^2b}{ab}\left[-\frac{(1-t)^3}{3}\right]_0^1 = \frac{a}{3} \\ \overline{y} = \frac{1}{ab}\int_0^1 ab^2t^2\, dt = \frac{ab^2}{ab}\left[\frac{t^3}{3}\right]_0^1 = \frac{b}{3} \text{.} \end{gather*}
