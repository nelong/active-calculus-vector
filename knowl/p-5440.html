For \(\overline{x}\text{,}\) we need \(\partial F_2/\partial x - \partial F_1/\partial y = x/A\text{.}\) It works nicely to let \(F_1 = 0\text{,}\) in which case taking \(\vF = \frac{1}{2A}x^2\vj\) gives what we need. For \(\overline{y}\text{,}\) we need \(\partial F_2/\partial x - \partial F_1/\partial y = y/A\text{.}\) It works nicely to let \(F_2 = 0\text{,}\) in which case taking \(\vG = -\frac{1}{2A}y^2\vi\) gives what we need.
