For a constant scalar \(k\text{,}\) scalar valued functions\(f\) and \(g\text{,}\) and oriented surfaces \(S_1\)and \(S_2\text{,}\) the following properties hold:
  1. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle \iint_{S_1} (k f) \, dS = k \iint_{S_1} f \, dS\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\iint_{S_1} (f+g) \, dS = \iint_{S_1} f dS + \iint_{S_1} g \, dS \)
  3. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\iint_{-S_1} f \, dS = \iint_{S_1} f \, dS\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \displaystyle\iint_{S_1+S_2} f \, dS = \iint_{S_1} f \, dS + \iint_{S_2} f \, dS\)
