Let \(\vu\) and \(\vv\) be vectors in \(\R^5\) with \(\vu \cdot \vv = -1\text{,}\) \(| \vu | = 2\text{,}\) \(| \vv | = 3\text{.}\) Use the properties of the dot product to find each of the following.

  1. \(\displaystyle \vu \cdot 2\vv\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \vv \cdot \vv\)

  3. \(\displaystyle (\vu + \vv) \cdot \vv\)

  4. \(\displaystyle (2\vu+4\vv) \cdot (\vu - 7\vv)\)

  5. \(|\vu| |\vv| \cos(\theta)\text{,}\) where \(\theta\) is the angle between \(\vu\) and \(\vv\)

  6. \(\displaystyle \theta\)
