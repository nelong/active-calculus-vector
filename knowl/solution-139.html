Solution 12.3.5.3.b.1.

We will parametrize \(C_3\) by \(\vr(t) = \langle 7\cos(t),7\sin(t) \rangle\) as \(0\leq t\leq 2\pi \text{.}\) So,

\begin{equation*} \int_{C_3} M\enspace x+N\enspace dy=\int_0^{2\pi} \langle -7\sin(t),7\cos(t)\rangle\cdot \langle-7\sin(t),7\cos(t)\rangle \enspace dt= 98\pi \text{.} \end{equation*}
