Solution 9.1.8.2.1.

SOLUTION
1. The contours have equation \(k = x^2- 5 y^2\) , which represent hyperbolas in the \(xy\)- plane.
2. The contours have equation \(k = x^2+ 2 y^2\) , which (for \(k >0\)) represent ellipses in the \(xy\)- plane.
3. The contours have equation \(k = y-3 x^2\) , which represent parabolas in the \(xy\)- plane.
4. The contours have equation \(k = -5 x^2\text{.}\) For \(k \le 0\text{,}\) these equations are equivalent to \(x = \pm \sqrt{-k/5}\text{,}\) which represent vertical lines in the \(xy\)- plane.
