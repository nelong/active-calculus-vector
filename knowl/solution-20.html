Solution 9.5.5.9.1.

(a) The time in minutes to go 10 miles at a speed of \(s\) mph is \((10/s)(60)= 600/s\text{.}\) Thus the 140 lb person going 10 mph uses \((8.3)(600/10) = 498\) calories, and the 200 lb person going 9 mph uses \((9.5)(600/9) = 633.333\) calories. (b) The 140 lb person burns \(498/140 = 3.55714\) calories per pound for the trip, while the 200 lb person burns \(633.333/200 = 3.16667\) calories per pound for the trip.
