Solution 12.5.4.a.1.
The scalar line integral \(\int_C f \, ds\) would calculate the accumulation of copium mined along the path given by \(C\) where \(f\) gives the density of copium at each location in the area. In particular, \(\int_C f \, ds\) measures the total copium mined along the path \(C\text{.}\)
