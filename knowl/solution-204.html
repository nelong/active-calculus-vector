Solution 12.6.4.3.1.

(a) \(\mbox{div}\,\left(\frac{y\vec i - x\vec j}{(x^2 + y^2)^{4}}\right)\) is a scalar (b) We have
\begin{equation*} \mbox{div}\,\left(\frac{y\vec i - x\vec j}{(x^2 + y^2)^{4}}\right) = \frac{-8xy}{\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)^{5}} + \frac{8xy}{\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)^{5}} = 0. \end{equation*}
