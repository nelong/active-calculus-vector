Solution 12.7.6.2.1.

\begin{equation*} \mbox{curl}(7z\,\mathit{\vec i}+6y\,\mathit{\vec j}+x\,\mathit{\vec k}) = \left| \begin{array}{ccc} \quad\vec i\quad \amp \quad \vec j\quad \amp \quad \vec k\quad \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \amp \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \amp \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\\ 7z \amp 6y \amp x \end{array} \right| = 6\,\mathit{\vec j}. \end{equation*}
