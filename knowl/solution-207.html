Solution 12.7.6.1.1.

(a) \(\mbox{div}\,\left(\frac{-y\vec i + x\vec j}{(x^2 + y^2)}\right)\) is a scalar (b) We have
\begin{equation*} \mbox{div}\,\left(\frac{-y\vec i + x\vec j}{(x^2 + y^2)}\right) = \frac{2xy}{\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)^{2}} + \frac{-2xy}{\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)^{2}} = 0. \end{equation*}
