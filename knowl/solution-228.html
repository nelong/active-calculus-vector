(b) The integrand of the integral over the disk \(R\) is \(96 - 6(x^2+y^2)\text{.}\) Since the integrand is positive for \(x^2 + y^2 \lt \frac{96}{6} = 16\) and negative for \(x^2 + y^2 > 16\text{,}\) the integrand is positive inside the circle of radius 4 and negative outside that circle. Thus, the integral over \(R\) increases with \(a\) until \(a=4\) and then decreases. The maximum value of the line integral occurs when \(a = 4\text{.}\)
