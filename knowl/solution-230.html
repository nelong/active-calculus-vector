Solution 12.9.4.1.1.

\(z + 4 x = 4\text{.}\) So \(4\vec i+ \vec k\) is a normal vector and \(\frac{1}{\sqrt 17}(4 \vec i+ \vec k)\) is a unit normal vector. Since this points in both the positive \(x\)-direction and the positive \(z\)-direction, it is cirrectly oriented for this surface. Since the area of the rectangle is \(\sqrt{17}\text{,}\) we have \(\vec A = 4\vec i + \vec k\) and \(\vec{v}\cdot\vec{A} = (-2\,\mathit{\vec i}-2\,\mathit{\vec j}-5\,\mathit{\vec k})\cdot(4\vec i + \vec k) = -13\text{.}\)
