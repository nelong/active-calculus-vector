Solution 10.6.8.7.1.

(a) In the direction of \(\nabla F\text{:}\)
\begin{equation*} \nabla F \bigg|_{(1,1,-1)} = \left((6x+4xz^{2})\vec i + 16y^{3} \vec j + 4x^{2}z \vec k \right)\bigg|_{(1,1,-1)}= 10\vec i + 16 \vec j - 4 \vec k. \end{equation*}
(b) The rate of change in the direction of \(\nabla F\) with respect to distance \(= ||\nabla F|| = \sqrt{372}\text{.}\) Now we want rate of change with respect to time. If we move at 7 cm/sec:
\begin{equation*} \mbox{Rate of change of } \frac{\mbox{Conc}}{\mbox{Time}} = \mbox{Rate of change of } \frac{\mbox{Conc}}{\mbox{Dist}} \times \mbox{Rate of change of } \frac{\mbox{Dist}}{\mbox{Time}} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = 7 \sqrt{(372)} \mbox{ mg/cm}^3/\mbox{sec}. \end{equation*}
