Solution 10.7.5.1.1.

\(D = k_{xx} k_{yy} - (k_{xy})^2\text{.}\) Here,
\begin{equation*} D = (- \cos( x) e^{-y^2})(\cos( x) (-2 e^{y^2} + 4y^2 e^{y^2})) - (2 y \sin( x) e^{y^2})^2, \end{equation*}
so that at the origin, \(D = 2\text{.}\) Thus \(D> 0\text{,}\) and \(f_{xx} \lt 0\text{,}\) so \(k(0,0)\) is a maximum.
