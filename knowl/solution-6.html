Solution 9.2.8.2.1.

SOLUTION
The given vector has length \(\left| \langle -6, 9, 6 \rangle \right| = \sqrt{(-6)^2+9^2+6^2}= \sqrt{153}\text{,}\) so a unit vector in the direction of \(\langle -6, 9, 6 \rangle\) is \(\mathbf u = \frac{1}{\sqrt{153}}\langle -6, 9, 6 \rangle\text{.}\)
A vector in the same direction but with length \(4\) is
\(\displaystyle 4 \mathbf u = \frac{4}{\sqrt{153}} \langle -6, 9, 6 \rangle = \left \langle \frac{-24}{\sqrt{153}} , \frac{36}{\sqrt{153}}, \frac{24}{\sqrt{153}} \right \rangle\text{.}\)
