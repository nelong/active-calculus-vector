Solution 11.3.3.6.1.

SOLUTION

The function \(f(x,y) = 33-x^{2}-y^{2}\) intersects the plane \(z = 8\) when \(x^2 + y^2 = 25\text{.}\)

Thus the region of integration is

\(\begin{array}{lcl} D \amp =\amp \left \lbrace (x,y) \, | \, -5 \le x \le 5, -\sqrt{25-x^2} \le y \le \sqrt{25-x^2} \right\rbrace \\ \amp =\amp \left \lbrace (x,y) \, | \, -5 \le y \le 5, -\sqrt{25-y^2} \le x \le \sqrt{25-y^2} \right\rbrace \end{array}\text{.}\)

The volume is then

\(V= \int_{-5}^{5} \int_{-\sqrt{25-x^2}}^{\sqrt{25-x^2}} \left( 33-x^2-y^2 - 8 \right) \, dy \, dx\) or

\(V= \int_{-5}^{5} \int_{-\sqrt{25-y^2}}^{\sqrt{25-y^2}} \left( 33-x^2-y^2 - 8 \right) \, dx \, dy\text{.}\)
