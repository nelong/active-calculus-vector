\begin{equation*} x = s\cos t, \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} y = s\sin t, \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} z = 2 ( 1 - \frac{1}{3}\, s ), \end{equation*}
with
\begin{equation*} 0 \le s\le 3 \quad\mbox{and}\quad 0\le t\le 2\pi. \end{equation*}
There are, of course, other parameterizations, but they will not be in terms of \(r\) and \(\theta\text{,}\) as required in this problem.
