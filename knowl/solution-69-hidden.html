Such vectors are perpendicular to the normal vector to the plane, \(\vec n = \left\lt 3,2,3\right>\text{.}\) We can choose any vectors \(\vec v_1\) and \(\vec v_2\) such that \(\vec v_1\cdot \vec n = \vec v_2\cdot \vec n = 0\text{.}\) One choice is
\begin{equation*} \vec v_1 = \left\lt -2,3,0\right>, \qquad \vec v_2 = \left\lt -3,0,3\right>. \end{equation*}
