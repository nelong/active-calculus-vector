Solution 11.6.4.5.1.

\(\phi = s\) and \(\theta = t\) as the two parameters. Since the radius is 6, we can take
\begin{equation*} x = 6\cos\!\left(t\right)\sin\!\left(s\right), \quad y = 6\sin\!\left(t\right)\sin\!\left(s\right), \quad \mbox{and}\quad z = 6\cos\!\left(s\right), \end{equation*}
with
\begin{equation*} 0\le s\le\pi\quad\mbox{and}\quad 0\le t\le 2\pi. \end{equation*}
