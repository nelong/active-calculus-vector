Solution 11.8.6.12.1.

\(r\) first, we have
\begin{equation*} \mbox{mass} = \int_0^{2\pi}\int_0^{5}\int_0^{4}(3+r)r\,dr\,dz\,d\theta = \int_0^{2\pi}\int_0^{5} \left(3\frac{r^2}2+\frac{r^3}3 \right) \Bigg|_0^{4}\,dz\,d\theta \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = 2\pi \cdot 5\cdot \left(\frac{48}2+\frac{64}3\right) = \frac{1360}{3}\pi\mbox{ g}. \end{equation*}
