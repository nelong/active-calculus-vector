part 13.3.4.c.
Let \(C_3\) be the circle of radius \(3\) centered at the origin, oriented counterclockwise. Calculate \(\oint_{C_3} \vF\cdot d\vr\text{.}\)
Solution.
In this part, the curve \(C_3\) is traced out by \(\vr(t) =\langle 3\cos(t),3\sin(t) \rangle\) as \(0 \leq t\leq 2\pi\text{.}\) So
\begin{equation*} \int_{C_3} \vF\cdot d\vr =\int_{0}^{2\pi} \langle 9(\sin(t))^2,2\cos(t)\sin(t)+3 \rangle \cdot \langle -3\sin(t),3\cos(t) \rangle \, dt = 0 \end{equation*}
