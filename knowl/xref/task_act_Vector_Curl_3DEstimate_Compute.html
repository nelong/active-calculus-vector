part 13.7.3.f.
Compute the curl of \(\vF=\langle x-y,y+2z,x^2\rangle\text{.}\) Specifically, what is \(\curl(\vF)\) at the point \((1,1,-2)\text{?}\)
Solution.
\(\curl(\vF)= (0-2)\vi -(2x-0)\vj+(0-1)\vk = \langle -2,-2x,-1 \rangle \text{,}\) so \(\curl(\vF)(1,1,-2)=\langle -2,-2,-1 \rangle \)
