part 13.11.3.b.
Find the circulation of \(\vG = 3z^2 \vi -(z^2 + 2x) \vj +zy \vk\) along the circle in the \(xy\)-plane of radius \(3\) centered at the origin. Assume the counterclockwise orientation of the circle.
Solution.
For the vector field \(\vG = 3z^2\vi -(z^2 + 2x) \vj+zy\vk\text{,}\) the curl is given by \(\langle z+2z,6z-0,2-0\rangle\text{.}\) We will use the disc of radius 3 in the xy-plane centered at the origin for our surface, with normal vector \(s \langle 0,0,1\rangle \text{.}\) Thus the flux through our surface will be two times the area of the surface, \(2 (9\pi)=18\pi\text{.}\)
